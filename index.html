Flashcross is an active outdoor game where teams of cyclists compete in travel strategy and solving photo-puzzles: the reproduction of pictures of objects. Everybody can join and win a Flashcross game. Whether you're good at cycling, orienteering, solving puzzles, or in just enjoying a nice sunday ride!

Flashcross 1: Tulip Expression

It's spring; a perfect time for enjoying flower fields at the first Dutch Flashcross game! Bring out your bike, catch some tailwind, and head out for new adventures!

The first game will take place on the 3rd of May from 09:30 to 16:00. It will take you through beautiful Noord Kennemerland in Noord Holland. This time of year tulips and other flowers for which the area is famous for will be blooming in the fields, so it should be quite the ride.

The starting location will be in Heiloo. From there you'll travel throughout the area trying to find and reproduce all the photo-puzzles within 4 hours. The finish is at 14:00, and results will be known at 16:00.

So choose a friend, bring your bikes to Heiloo on the 3rd of May, and explore Noord Kennemerland! Regardless of whether you just like to ride or enjoy challenging competitions, it'll be fun!
