Flashcross is a photo reproduction game where teams of cyclists compete to reproduce pictures of places and objects. Anybody can join a Flashcross game. Whether you're good at cycling, orienteering, solving puzzles, or just enjoy a nice sunday ride!

Flashcross 1: Tulip Expression

It's spring; a perfect time for the first Dutch Flashcross game! Bring out your bike, catch some tailwind, and head out for new adventures!

The first game will take place on the 3rd of May from 09:30 to 14:30. It will take you through beautiful Noord Kennemerland in Noord Holland.

The starting location will be in Heiloo. From there you'll travel throughout area trying to reproduce all the photos. This time of year tulips and other flowers for which the area is famous for will be blooming in the fields, so it should be quite the ride.

So find a friend and bring your bikes to Heiloo on the 3rd of may and explore Noord Kennemerland! Regardless of whether you just like to ride or enjoy solving puzzles, it'll be fun!
